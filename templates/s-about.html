We look after the bits left behind

Located in Medford Oregon, AfterBits Electronic Recycling was established in an effort to help cultivate a more sustainable solution to our ever growing e-waste problem in Southern Oregon.

We accomplish this through our primary objective of upcycling products, not harvesting raw materials through harmful chemicals or dangerous processes.

By doing so, we are able to undue some of the damage already done to our Earth by properly repurposing e-waste and keeping it out of landfills.
